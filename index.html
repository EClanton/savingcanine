Our Story

Saving Canine started when Decatur residents started seeing an influx of stray animals roaming, he streets. This didn’t feel right to me, and I knew something needed to be done. That’s why I started Saving Canine. We strive to get more animals off the streets and into loving homes.

We are located in Decatur Illinois and you can contact us with any questions or to let us know you've spotted a stray at savingcanine@gmail.com









Saving Canine is here to help the Decatur community. Visit our adoption page to find your FUREVER friend today!
